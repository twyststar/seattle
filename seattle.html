Moving to Seattle

Adventures in Cross-Country Relocations

Here are two impressions of moving to Seattle. One of us moved from Philly, one from Salt Lake.

Making the cross country trek was exciting, and a little scary. Coming from an East coast city like Philly proved to be a stark change of pace.

Moving from a sleepy desert town to a vibrant coastal city was a huge change. The climate, both physical and political, could not be more different.
